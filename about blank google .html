About:Blank Cloaker

Enter a search query or URL (starting with "https://") to open it in a new about:blank tab.

• Your actions here will not be saved in your search history.
• Clicking links will keep them opening in the same about:blank tab.

Note: This is not a proxy.
• Searches that are normally blocked will be unblocked.
• However, links from those searches or URLs that are blocked by your network will remain blocked.
• Sites that do not allow iframes will not work.
